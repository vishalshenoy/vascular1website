About Vascular1

Vascular1 is a virtual reality based module designed to train essential hand-eye coordination skills for ultrasound guided vascular access (UGVA) procedures. The module immerses trainees in an operation theatre and prompts them to perform UGVA on the common femoral artery. An ultrasound system and life-like surgical tools are rendered in the virtual reality environment. Vascular1 operates on the Oculus Quest VR headset.
